The SwapScholar model

One platform, two ways to learn.

Arrange a direct skill swap or use time credits when a mutual match is not available.

⇄

Mutual skill swap

Teach each other.

You offer a skill the other student wants and request one of theirs in return. No credits are used.

  • Best for mutual matches
  • Both students are teacher and learner
  • One shared completion workflow
◆

Time-credit lesson

Keep value moving.

Spend one credit to learn. After both students confirm completion, the teacher earns that credit.

  • Every account starts with two credits
  • Useful when no direct swap exists
  • Credits transfer only after completion

Built for student data

A small but real backend application.

Accounts, password hashes, profiles, requests, status changes, credits and reviews are stored in SQLite on the Azure server. Server-side validation and permission checks protect each workflow.
