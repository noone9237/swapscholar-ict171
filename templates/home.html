Is SwapScholar free to use?+

Yes. The platform is designed for student-to-student learning through mutual exchanges and time credits, not paid tutoring.

How does a mutual match work?+

A mutual match appears when you offer something another student wants and they offer something you want.

When is a time credit transferred?+

The credit transfers only after both students mark the accepted exchange as completed.

Can anyone leave a review?+

No. Only students who participated in a completed exchange can review one another.

What happens after I create an account?+

Add your profile and skills, browse or open your recommended matches, then send your first exchange request.
